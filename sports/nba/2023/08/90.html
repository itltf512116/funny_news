USC Trojans: LeBron James, Family Reveal Real Reason for Bronny James' Cardiac Arrest

LeBron James of the Los Angeles Lakers has revealed the real reason behind the shocking cardiac arrest of his son Bronny James last July 24 during practice with the USC Trojans.

The 38-year-old star said that after evaluations done by doctors from Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, the Mayo Clinic, and Atlantic Health/ Morristown Medical Center, the probable cause of the USC Trojans freshman's sudden cardiac arrest was an anatomically and functionally significant Congenital Heart Defect, Shams Charania of The Athletic reported.

Congenital heart defect was the cause of Bronny James’ cardiac arrest on July 24 and there is confidence he will make a full recovery and return to basketball in the very near future, per statement from James family spokesperson.
