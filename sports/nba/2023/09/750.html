NBA: Magic Johnson Admits Interest in Team Ownership Stake — It's Not the Lakers

01:21+08:00, AM. Sep. 28, 2023 By Jeremiah Santos
(Photo : Meg Oliphant/Getty Images) EL SEGUNDO, CA - Earvin "Magic" Johnson discusses the upcoming Los Angeles Lakers' season at UCLA Health Training Center on September 20, 2018 in El Segundo, California.

Los Angeles Lakers legend Magic Johnson confessed that his deep affection for the purple and gold franchise has always prevented him from contemplating ownership of any other NBA team. 

However, there is one particular team that could pique his interest.

"I think it would be intriguing. The only team I would actually probably think about is the New York Knicks," the five-time NBA champion said.

The Laker great thinks that the way Knicks fans are passionate about the game might get him to consider holding a stake with the team.

The three-time NBA Most Valuable Player also said he loves visiting New York and Madison Square Garden to watch the team play.

Johnson  he loves being with fans who are passionate about their team, and Knicks fans are among them. The twelve-time NBA All-Star described the fanbase as "smart." 

"I think that other than that, I would probably never think about ever being a part of another franchise," Johnson concluded.

NBA teams that Magic Johnson rejected  

Johnson disclosed  with three NBA teams mainly to avoid direct competition with his beloved Lakers. 

These are the Golden State Warriors, Detroit Pistons, and Atlanta Hawks.

However, he is not shy about holding significant stakes on teams in other sports, such as the MLB's Los Angeles Dodgers and the NFL's Washington Commanders.
