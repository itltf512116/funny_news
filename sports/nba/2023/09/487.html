Houston Rockets guard Kevin Porter Jr.  in New York City for allegedly assaulting his girlfriend, former WNBA player Kysre Gondrezick, at a hotel on Monday morning.

The 23-year-old Rockets player was officially charged with assault and strangulation, both felonies.

Kevin Porter Jr's girlfriend was a former WNBA lottery pick

Porter's partner, former WNBA player Kysre Gondrezick, was the alleged victim of the incident.

The Indiana Fever selected the 26-year-old with the fourth overall pick in the 2021 WNBA Draft. She spent her rookie year in Indiana  in January 2022. 

She signed with the Chicago Sky for the team's 2022 training camp but was released before the season started.

Gondrezick has not played the last two WNBA seasons and is currently a free agent. She played college basketball for Michigan and West Virginia.
