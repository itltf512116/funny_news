Real Madrid: Jude Bellingham Better Than Erling Haaland, Kylian Mbappe, Claims Kepa

Who is the best young player in the world right now? 

Is it the Erling Haaland who just won his treble on his first try in the Premier League? Is he the best player on his team?

Is it Kylian Mbappe who won the World Cup and the last FIFA World Cup Golden Boot Award?

None of those two for Real Madrid goalkeeper Kepa. For him is tearing teams apart in La Liga and the Champions League.

"Right now, on a global level, I don't know if I see anyone above Bellingham," he told reporters.

Kepa also took into account the defensive prowess of his young teammate, who  Borussia Dortmund.
