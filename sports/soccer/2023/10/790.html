Inter Miami Lose US Open Cup Final Without Lionel Messi

12:16+08:00, AM. Oct. 02, 2023 By Jeremiah Santos
(Photo : Carmen Mandato/Getty Images) FORT LAUDERDALE, FLORIDA - Lionel Messi (#10) of Inter Miami reacts during the first half of their match against Toronto FC at DRV PNK Stadium on September 20, 2023 in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.

Inter Miami failed to capture their second trophy in club history,  Houston Dynamo, 2-1, in the Lamar Hunt US Open Cup Final held in their home at the DRV PNK Stadium in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. 

Argentine icon Lionel Messi and Spanish left-back Jordi Alba were both sidelined due to injuries, while summer signing Sergio Busquets played in the game.

"It was clearly not prudent for him to play, not even for a few minutes. We couldn't take the risk. But he will surely play again before the end of the season," Inter Miami manager Tata Martino said after the loss. 

Before this game, Messi had already missed three of Miami's last four games due to muscle fatigue. 

The Argentine tactician  Messi's availability will be determined on a game-by-game basis, based upon the feedback from the medical team regarding his injury. 

Inter Miami are looking to rise up the MLS standings to qualify for the playoffs, where they are currently in 14th place in the Eastern Conference with 32 points.
