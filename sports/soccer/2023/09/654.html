Inter Miami star Lionel Messi missed the team's past two MLS games against Sporting KC and Atlanta United after  one of Argentina's 2026 FIFA World Cup qualifying matches during the international break over a week ago, and resting afterwards after due to "muscle fatigue."

The Fort Lauderdale-based club will have the Toronto FC next in the calendar on Wednesday at the DRV PNK Stadium.

Will Lionel Messi play in the crucial game that will impact their playoff hopes?

Inter Miami's Lionel Messi Injury Status
