Manchester United GK Andre Onana Takes Blame After Team's Brutal Champions League Defeat and Losing Skid

Manchester United goalkeeper Andre Onana  on Wednesday, September 19, after the Red Devils suffered a 4-3 loss against Bayern Munich in the UEFA Champions League group stages held at the Allianz Arena.
