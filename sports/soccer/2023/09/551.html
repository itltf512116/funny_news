Inter Miami: Here's How Much Lionel Messi Paid for Waterfront Mansion in Miami

Inter Miami star Lionel Messi has acquired a waterfront property in the gated Bay Colony neighborhood of Fort Lauderdale, Florida,  real estate news outlet The Real Deal.

The World Cup winner and his wife, Antonella Roccuzzo,  10,500-square-foot, eight-bedroom estate at 91 Compass Lane for a staggering price of $10.8 million.

The home is five miles and a 15-minute drive from Inter Miami's home, DRV PNK Stadium, and the team's training facility adjacent to the playing venue.

John and Stacy Dickerson were the previous owners of the home, and they bought the whole place for $9 million in May 2022, giving them a 20 percent profit in 16 months upon the transaction with the global star.

Messi officially joined Inter Miami last July and has been spotted searching for a house across South Florida from Fort Lauderdale to Boca Raton.
